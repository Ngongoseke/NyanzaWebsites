Hosting and Domain Details

Every website needs a place to live online, called hosting. Hosting requires monthly fees depending on the type of hosting and domain name you choose.

Free Hosting:

Your site will have a default domain name (e.g., example.mywebsitehost.com). You won't be able to choose a custom domain, but it's a good way to get started without extra costs.

Paid Hosting:

Allows you to choose a custom domain name (e.g., www.example.com) that represents your brand better. This requires purchasing a domain, starting at approximately $10-$20 per year, plus hosting fees.

Initially, I will host your website for free. If you decide you want a custom domain later, I can assist you in setting it up.

Why Invest in a Custom Domain?

Custom domains give your website a professional and trustworthy appearance, helping to establish credibility and brand identity. They also allow for better search engine optimization (SEO) and email addresses linked to your domain.

If you're not ready to commit to a custom domain, starting with a free domain is a great way to test your website and see how it performs.

When you're ready, upgrading is seamless, and I'll guide you through the process of choosing and purchasing the perfect domain name for your needs.
